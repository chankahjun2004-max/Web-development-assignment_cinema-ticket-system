Ticket Booking

How do I book tickets online?

You can book tickets online through our website or in person at our cinema.

Can I cancel or change my booking?

You can cancel your bookings a minimum of 3 days before the showing time.

Are there any discounts available for bulk or group bookings?

We do have discounts available every now and then, be sure to keep your eyes peeled in our social media accounts.

How far in advance can I book tickets?

You can book tickets up to 30 days in advance.

Pricing and Payment

What are the ticket prices for adults, children, and seniors?

Our tickets prices vary from on Weekdays. Weekends and seating types. Please check our website for the latest prices.

Do you offer any membership discounts or loyalty programs?

We do have a loyalty program for our customers, you can find more information in our social media accounts.

What payment methods are accepted at the cinema and online?

We accept all major credit cards and debit cards, as well as PayPal and Apple Pay.

Are there any additional fees for online booking?

We do not charge any additional fees for online booking.

Showtimes and Availability

How can I check movie showtimes?

You can check via our website and the email we sent you when you confirm your booking

What do I do if a show is sold out?

You can book a ticket for the next available show.

How often do you update your movie listings?

We update our movie listings every week.

Cinema Facilities

What types of screens and seating options are available?

We offer a variety of screens including standard, IMAX, and 4DX. Seating options range from regular to premium recliners and VIP seats with additional legroom and comfort.

Are your facilities wheelchair accessible?

Yes, our cinema is fully accessible to wheelchair users, including accessible restrooms and dedicated seating areas in each theater.

Do you provide hearing-impaired services?

We provide hearing-impaired services such as subtitled screenings and special headphones upon request.

What are the food and drink options at the cinema?

Our concession stands offer a range of snacks including popcorn, nachos, hotdogs, as well as a selection of soft drinks, juices, and Mineral Water.

Special Screenings and Events

Do you host special events or movie premieres?

Yes, we host special events and movie premieres. Check our website or subscribe to our newsletter for upcoming events.

How can I find out about special screenings and events?

Information about special screenings and events can be found on our website, social media channels, or by subscribing to our newsletter.

Are private screenings available for booking?

Private screenings can be booked for special occasions. Please contact our customer service for more details and availability.

Policies and Safety

What is your policy on refunds and exchanges?

Refunds and exchanges are available under certain conditions. Please review our refund policy on our website or contact customer service for assistance.

What are the cinema's policies regarding children and age restrictions for movies?

Age restrictions for movies are in accordance with film rating systems. Children under age 12 must be accompanied by an adult for PG-13 movies.

What safety measures are in place at the cinema?

We adhere to strict safety protocols including regular cleaning, security checks, and compliance with local health guidelines to ensure our guests' safety.

Technology and Features

Do you offer 3D and IMAX screenings?

Yes, we offer both 3D and IMAX screenings. Check our showtimes for available formats.

What are the benefits of watching a movie in IMAX or 3D?

IMAX provides an immersive viewing experience with sharper images and superior sound quality, while 3D movies offer a depth perception feature that enhances the visual experience.

Are subtitles available for non-English movies or for the hearing impaired?

Yes, we provide subtitles for non-English movies and also offer special screenings with subtitles for the hearing impaired.

Customer Support

How can I contact customer service if I have an issue?

Customer service can be contacted via our website's contact form, by phone, or by visiting the customer service desk at our cinema.

What are the hours of operation for customer support?

Our customer support is available from 10 AM to 10 PM daily.

Where can I provide feedback about my experience?

Feedback can be provided through our website's feedback form, directly at the cinema, or via our social media pages.

Miscellaneous

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

No, outside food and drinks are not permitted. However, you can purchase a variety of snacks and beverages at our concession stands.

Is there parking available at the cinema? Is it free?

Parking is available and free for cinema patrons. The parking lot is monitored for security and convenience.

What should I do if I lost an item at the cinema?

Please report any lost items to our customer service desk or contact us through our website. We maintain a lost and found section and will do our best to help you recover your item.
